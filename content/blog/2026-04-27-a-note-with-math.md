This template supports inline math like $f(x)=x^2+1$ and display math:

$$
\min_{w} \; \mathcal{L}(w) = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\ell(x_i, y_i; w) + \lambda \lVert w \rVert_2^2
$$

You can also write quick reasoning notes:

$$
\nabla_w \mathcal{L}(w) = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\nabla_w \ell(x_i, y_i; w) + 2\lambda w
$$

That makes the blog usable for:

- derivations
- reading notes
- paper summaries
- teaching material drafts
