Redwood Scholar is built for researchers who want their homepage to feel like more than a CV with links.

It tries to connect three layers:

1. Papers and formal results.
2. Notes and half-polished ideas.
3. A readable public face that still feels personal.

One small principle behind the design is this:

> academic websites should be easy to maintain, but they should not feel emotionally flat.

The template is configuration-driven, but it still leaves space for rhythm, voice, and a little surprise.
